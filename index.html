My Approach

Cutting-Edge Learning: The AI-Augmented Model

My approach reflects the latest research in education, which shows that the most effective tutoring utilizes an AI-augmented model: an approach that pairs a dedicated, human mentor with smart, responsive AI technology to give your student the best of both worlds.

The Joint Attention Tutoring System

I implement this augmented model by combining regular one-on-one mentorship with a custom, data-driven system centered around Google's LearnLM. This approach pairs today's most advanced tools with time-tested educational benchmarks; all methods are based entirely on established Common Core standards.

This system helps us pinpoint the exact concepts your student needs to focus on, allowing me to create a 100% personalized plan that adapts after every session.

Here’s what that means for your student:

No More Guesswork: We scientifically identify specific learning gaps and persistent struggles, so we're not wasting time on concepts they've already mastered.

A Clear Path Forward: Your student gets an automatically updated study plan focused on the most important skills they need to learn next.

Visible Proof of Progress: You'll receive clear, easy-to-understand reports that visually track your student's growth. You will see the wins as they happen!

Support Beyond Our Sessions: Your student gets access to safe, 24/7 AI-guided practice tools that are perfectly aligned with the personal goals we set together.

This system handles the heavy data-lifting, which frees me to focus on the most important part: human-first mentor, building your student's confidence, and helping them foster a genuine curiosity for the subject and for learning in general.

Traditional Tutoring

While the data-augmented approach provides a great deal of value and clarity, I understand that every student is different. If you are looking for a more traditional, 100% person-to-person online tutoring experience without the data system, I am always happy to offer these as well.


My goal is to find the perfect fit for your student's success.
