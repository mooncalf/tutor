My Approach

Cutting-Edge Learning

My approach reflects the latest research in education, which shows that the most effective tutoring utilizes a hybrid model: an approach that pairs a dedicated, human mentor with smart, responsive technology. This provides the learner with the best of both worlds.

I implement this hybrid model by combining regular one-on-one mentorship or tutoring sessions with a custom, data-driven system based entirely on established Common Core standards for education.

This system helps us work together to pinpoint the exact concepts your student needs to focus on. It allows me to create a 100% personalized plan that adapts after every session.

Here’s what that means for your student:

  • No More Guesswork: We scientifically identify specific learning gaps and persistent struggles, so we're not wasting time on concepts they've already mastered.
  • A Clear Path Forward: Your student gets an automatically updated study plan focused on the most important skills they need to learn next.
  • Visible Proof of Progress: You'll receive clear, easy-to-understand reports that visually track your student's growth. You will see the wins as they happen!
  • Support Beyond Our Sessions: Your student gets access to safe, 24/7 AI-guided practice tools that are perfectly aligned with the personal goals we set together.

This system handles the heavy data-lifting, which frees me to focus on the most important part: human-first mentor, building your student's confidence, and helping them foster a genuine curiosity for the subject and for learning in general.

Traditional Tutoring

This data-augmented approach provides a great deal of value and clarity, but I understand that every student is different. If you are looking for a more traditional, 100% person-to-person online tutoring experience without the data system, I am always happy to offer these as well.


My goal is to find the perfect fit for your student's success.
